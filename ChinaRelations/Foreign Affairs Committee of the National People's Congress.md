The **Foreign Affairs Committee of the National People's Congress** ([Chinese](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Simplified_Chinese_characters "Simplified Chinese characters"): 全国人民代表大会外事委员会; [pinyin](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pinyin "Pinyin"): *Quánguó Rénmín Dàibiǎo Dàhuì Wàishì Wěiyuánhuì*) is one of nine [special committees](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Special_committee_of_the_National_People%27s_Congress "Special committee of the National People's Congress") of the [National People's Congress](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/National_People%27s_Congress "National People's Congress") ([NPC](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/National_People%27s_Congress "National People's Congress")), the national legislature of the [People's Republic of China](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/People%27s_Republic_of_China "People's Republic of China"). The special committee was created during the first session of the [6th National People's Congress](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/6th_National_People%27s_Congress "6th National People's Congress") in June 1983, and has existed for every National People's Congress since.[^1]

Composed of a chairman, a number of vice-chairmen, and several members, the Foreign Affairs Committee is responsible for reviewing and deliberating on proposed [legislation](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Legislation "Legislation") regarding foreign affairs including the [signing](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ratification "Ratification") or abrogation of [treaties](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Treaty "Treaty") and agreements with other nations for delivery to the [NPC](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/National_People%27s_Congress "National People's Congress"), reviewing and replying to [inquiries](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Inquiry "Inquiry") submitted by the [NPC](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/National_People%27s_Congress "National People's Congress") regarding [foreign affairs](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Foreign_policy "Foreign policy"), communicate with foreign counterparts, make statements for major issues related to [Chinese foreign affairs](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Foreign_relations_of_China "Foreign relations of China"), present briefings to relevant departments on international issues, and all other tasks given it by the [National People's Congress](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/National_People%27s_Congress "National People's Congress").[^2]
