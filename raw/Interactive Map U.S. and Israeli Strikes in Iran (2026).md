### US and Israeli Strikes in Iran Definitions

**Confirmed Airstrike (Green)**: This denotes an airstrike that has been visually confirmed and corroborated by sources with accompanying photos or other verifiable information.

**Reported Airstrike (Blue)**: This denotes an airstrike that has been reported by a source with accompanying photos or other verifiable information.

**Report of Explosion with Footage (Yellow)**: This denotes a report of an unverified explosion with accompanying photos or video.

**Report of Explosion without Footage (Red)**: This denotes a report of an unverified explosion without accompanying photo or video.

### Iran and Axis of Resistance Response Definition

The Iran and Axis Response feature represents Iranian and Axis of Resistance retaliatory strikes against the US, Israel, and countries hosting US bases, and other targets. The feature consists of discrete kinetic events recorded by date, location, and actor.
