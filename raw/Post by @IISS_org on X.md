China’s evacuation of its citizens in response to recent conflicts in the Middle East has mainly relied on organising vehicles to take citizens from Iran, Iraq and Israel to land border crossings, such as the Taba crossing with Egypt and the Astara crossing between Iraq and Azerbaijan. However, after the start of the Israel–Hamas war, Chinese citizens were told to organise their own commercial flights back to China.
