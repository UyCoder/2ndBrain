"Those who provide the greatest benefit to rulers are those who can combine seriousness with humor and present the truth to them in an appropriate manner. Just as the most delicious dessert is the one that contains a bit of bread within it."

Presenting the truth on its own leaves one dry; presenting only sweetness chokes. The art of the advisor is to mix the two in the right proportion.

— (The Lion and the Jackal — Author Unknown Political Treatise, trans. Merve Karakaya, Klasik Yayınları, p.53)
