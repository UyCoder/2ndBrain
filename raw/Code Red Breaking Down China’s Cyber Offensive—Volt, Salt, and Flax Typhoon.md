China is already inside U.S. systems. That’s the message from the McCrary Institute’s latest report—and this Cyber Focus episode breaks it down. From Volt Typhoon to Salt and Flax, these cyber campaigns aren’t isolated attacks. They’re part of a coordinated strategy by the Chinese Communist Party to prepare the battlefield, exploit infrastructure gaps, and undermine U.S. resilience before a conflict even begins.  
  
Host Frank Cilluffo sits down with RADM Mark Montgomery (Ret.), Brad Medairy, and Bill Evanina—co-chairs of the report—to explain what China’s targeting, why they want us to know they’re there, and how the U.S. can push back. They also tackle deterrence failure, why AI is accelerating the threat, and what needs to change before it’s too late.
