Pakistan Says Terror Groups Supported by Afghan Taliban Are Operating in Afghanistan

Asim Iftikhar Ahmad, Permanent Representative of Pakistan to the United Nations, says that contrary to Pakistan’s expectations, terrorist groups such as TTP, BLA, ISIS, and ETIM are operating on Afghan soil under the support of the Taliban.

He mentions the increase in terrorist attacks in Pakistan and says that these attacks originate from Afghan soil.
