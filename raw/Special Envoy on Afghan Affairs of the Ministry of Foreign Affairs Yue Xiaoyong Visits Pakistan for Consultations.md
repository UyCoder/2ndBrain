[Ministry of Foreign Affairs  
People’s Republic of China](https://www.fmprc.gov.cn/eng/)From May 31 to June 3, 2026, Special Envoy on Afghan Affairs of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Yue Xiaoyong visited Pakistan and met respectively with Pakistani Foreign Secretary Amna Baloch and Pakistani Prime Minister’s Special Representative for Afghanistan Mohammad Sadiq, among others. They exchanged views on China-Pakistan relations and international and regional issues of common interest.
