### Chapter 3: The Saudi Arabia Of Uranium

**32:33** · Let's start with some numbers.

**32:35** · Kazakhstan currently produces over 40% of the entire world's uranium supply.

**32:40** · The major uranium mining regions are concentrated in the south of the country, primarily in two vast regions Turkestan and Kyzylorda.

**32:49** · Together, they produce roughly 70 tons of uranium every single day.
