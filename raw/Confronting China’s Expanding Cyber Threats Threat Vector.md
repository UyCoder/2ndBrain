In this episode of Threat Vector, host David Moulton (https://www.linkedin.com/in/davidrmoulton/) talks with Wendi Whitmore (https://www.linkedin.com/in/wendiwhitmore2/) , Chief Security Intelligence Officer at Palo Alto Networks, about the increasing scale of China-linked cyber threats and the vulnerabilities in outdated OT environments.   
  
  
  
Wendi shares critical insights on how nation-state threats have evolved, why AI must be part of modern defense strategies, and the importance of real-time intelligence sharing.
