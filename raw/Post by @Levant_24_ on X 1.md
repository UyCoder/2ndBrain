📌 The Syrian government plans to repurpose the Russian-controlled Hmeimim airbase into a training and humanitarian facility.

📌 The base is expected to lose its military role and come under joint Russian-Syrian oversight, with a shift toward non-combat functions.

📌 Damascus aims to use the site to train its armed forces. Syrian authorities are seeking Russian instructors, mainly due to the army’s reliance on Soviet- and Russian-made equipment, while limiting cooperation to ground-force training only.

📌 The base may still serve as a logistical transit hub for Russian operations to Africa, supporting cargo transfers despite reduced military activity.

📌 A full Russian withdrawal from Hmeimim is not ruled out, with Damascus potentially using such a move to declare the end of Russia’s military presence on Syria’s coast.

Source: Kommersant
