Energy Minister Mohammad al-Bashir on the sidelines of a roundtable at the US Chamber of Commerce:

📌 Discussions included strong interest from major US energy companies and international firms in investing and operating in Syria.

📌 Talks were also held with the US Energy Minister, reflecting interest from the United States in the energy sector.

📌 Financial and monetary transfer challenges affecting investment flows were discussed.

📌 A visit by a delegation of US companies, mainly from the energy sector and other fields, to Syria is planned for September.

📌 The meetings also covered governance reforms, restructuring of national companies, and amendments to investment laws aimed at facilitating investment.
