Western media is amplifying claims that Iran plans to charge major tech companies for use of undersea internet cables passing through the Strait of Hormuz and restrict maintenance to Iranian firms. Such a move could disrupt around 15% of global internet traffic.

The real vulnerability lies in the Bab el-Mandeb Strait, where Houthis have previously demonstrated the ability to target similar infrastructure.
