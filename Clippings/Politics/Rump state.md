Kingdom of Soissons, a Roman rump state following the collapse of the Western Roman Empire.

A **rump state** is the remnant of a once much larger [state](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/State_\(polity\) "State (polity)") that was reduced in the wake of [annexation](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Annexation "Annexation"), [occupation](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Military_occupation "Military occupation"), [secession](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Secession "Secession"), [decolonization](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Decolonization "Decolonization"), a successful [coup d'état](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Coup_d%27%C3%A9tat "Coup d'état") or [revolution](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Revolution "Revolution") on part of its former [territory](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Territory "Territory").[^1] In the last case, a government stops short of going into [exile](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Government_in_exile "Government in exile") because it controls parts of its remaining territories.[^2]

## Examples

### Ancient history

- During the [Second Intermediate Period](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Second_Intermediate_Period "Second Intermediate Period"), following the conquest of [Lower Egypt](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lower_Egypt "Lower Egypt") by the [Hyksos](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hyksos "Hyksos"), there was a rump Egyptian kingdom in [Upper Egypt](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Upper_Egypt "Upper Egypt") centered on [Thebes](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Thebes,_Egypt "Thebes, Egypt"), which eventually reunified the country at the start of the [New Kingdom](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/New_Kingdom_of_Egypt "New Kingdom of Egypt").[^3] [^4] [^5]
- The [Seleucid Empire](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Seleucid_Empire "Seleucid Empire") became a rump state in Northern Syria after losing most of its territory to the [Parthian Empire](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Parthian_Empire "Parthian Empire").[^6]
- After the collapse of the [Western Roman Empire](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Western_Roman_Empire "Western Roman Empire") in [Gaul](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gaul "Gaul"), the [Kingdom of Soissons](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kingdom_of_Soissons "Kingdom of Soissons") survived as a rump state under [Aegidius](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Aegidius "Aegidius") and [Syagrius](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Syagrius "Syagrius"), until it was conquered by the [Franks](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Francia "Francia") under [Clovis I](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Clovis_I "Clovis I") in 486.[^7]

### Post-classical history

- [Guge](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Guge "Guge") and [Maryul](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Maryul "Maryul") were rump states of the [Tibetan Empire](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tibetan_Empire "Tibetan Empire").[^8] [^9]
- The [Sultanate of Rum](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sultanate_of_Rum "Sultanate of Rum") was a rump state of the [Seljuk Empire](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Seljuk_Empire "Seljuk Empire").[^10]
- [Armenian Kingdom of Cilicia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Armenian_Kingdom_of_Cilicia "Armenian Kingdom of Cilicia") was an Armenian rump state in Cilicia.[^11]
- After the [Almoravid](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Almoravid "Almoravid") conquest of the [Taifa of Zaragoza](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Taifa_of_Zaragoza "Taifa of Zaragoza") in 1110, the taifa's last ruler, Abd-al-Malik, maintained a tiny rump emirate at [Rueda de Jalón](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Rueda_de_Jal%C3%B3n "Rueda de Jalón") until his death in 1130.[^12]
- [Qara Khitai](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Qara_Khitai "Qara Khitai") was a rump state of the [Liao dynasty](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Liao_dynasty "Liao dynasty").[^13]
- After the [Jin dynasty](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Jin_dynasty_\(1115%E2%80%931234\) "Jin dynasty (1115–1234)") assumed control over northern China in 1127, the [Southern Song](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Song_dynasty#Southern_Song,_1127%E2%80%931279 "Song dynasty") existed as a rump state of the Northern [Song dynasty](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Song_dynasty "Song dynasty"), although it still retained over half of Northern Song's territory and more than half of its population.[^14] [^15]
- Several [Byzantine](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Byzantine_Empire "Byzantine Empire") rump states like [Nicaea](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Empire_of_Nicaea "Empire of Nicaea"), [Trebizond](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Empire_of_Trebizond "Empire of Trebizond"), [Morea](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Despotate_of_the_Morea "Despotate of the Morea"), [Theodoro](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Principality_of_Theodoro "Principality of Theodoro") and [Epirus](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Despotate_of_Epirus "Despotate of Epirus") were formed following conquests from Muslim Turks and Crusaders.[^16] [^17] [^18]
- After the [Ming dynasty](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ming_dynasty "Ming dynasty") established control over [China proper](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/China_proper "China proper") in 1368, the [Yuan dynasty](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Yuan_dynasty "Yuan dynasty") retreated to the [Mongolian Plateau](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mongolian_Plateau "Mongolian Plateau") and survived as a rump state called the [Northern Yuan](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Northern_Yuan "Northern Yuan").[^19]
- After the [Fragmentation of the Golden Horde](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Great_Horde#Fragmentation_of_the_Golden_Horde "Great Horde") in the early 15th century, the [Great Horde](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Great_Horde "Great Horde") (first mentioned in the 1430s) survived as its rump state in the heartland of the former Khanate in lower Volga, until the [Crimean Khanate](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Crimean_Khanate "Crimean Khanate") destroyed it by sacking its capital city of [New Sarai](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/New_Sarai "New Sarai") in 1502, absorbing its remaining territory.
- The [Timurid Empire](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Timurid_Empire "Timurid Empire") reduced into a rump state in [Kabulistan](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kabulistan "Kabulistan") and [Balkh](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Balkh "Balkh") under [Babur](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Babur "Babur") after most of its territory in Khorasan and Central Asia falls to Shaybanid [Khanate of Bukhara](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Khanate_of_Bukhara "Khanate of Bukhara") in 1500s, the state later turned into the [Mughal Empire](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mughal_Empire "Mughal Empire") after the [Babur's conquest of Delhi](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/First_Battle_of_Panipat "First Battle of Panipat") in 1526.
- By summer 1503, [Aq Qoyunlu](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Aq_Qoyunlu "Aq Qoyunlu") rule collapsed in Iran. Some Aq Qoyunlu rump states continued to survive until 1508, before they were absorbed into the [Safavid Empire](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Safavid_Empire "Safavid Empire") by [Ismail I](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ismail_I "Ismail I").[^20]
- After the fall of the [Malacca Sultanate](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Malacca_Sultanate "Malacca Sultanate") in 1511 to the Portuguese naval forces, many of the Malaccan royalty and nobility retreated to the southern region of the [Malay Peninsula](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Malay_Peninsula "Malay Peninsula") and established the [Johor Sultanate](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Johor_Sultanate "Johor Sultanate").[^21]
- After the [Spanish conquest](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Spanish_conquest_of_the_Inca_Empire "Spanish conquest of the Inca Empire") of the [Inca Empire](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Inca_Empire "Inca Empire") in 1532, the [Neo-Inca State](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Neo-Inca_State "Neo-Inca State") based at [Vilcabamba](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Vilcabamba,_Peru "Vilcabamba, Peru") survived as a rump state until 1572.[^22]
- Despite having two lords, one of them the king of France, [Andorra](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Andorra "Andorra") was part of the [Principality of Catalonia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Principality_of_Catalonia "Principality of Catalonia") until the [Nueva Planta decrees](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Nueva_Planta_decrees "Nueva Planta decrees") (1715), after the [Catalan defeat](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/War_of_the_Catalans "War of the Catalans") in the [War of the Spanish Succession](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/War_of_the_Spanish_Succession "War of the Spanish Succession"). To avoid the application of Nueva Planta to Andorra, the [Bishop of Urgell](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Diocese_of_Urgell "Diocese of Urgell") (the other lord of Andorra) convinced the new [Spanish Bourbon](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/History_of_Spain_\(1700%E2%80%931808\) "History of Spain (1700–1808)") authorities that the Valleys of Andorra had always been neutral and unrelated to the Principality of Catalonia, resulting in the political separation of Andorra from [Catalonia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Catalonia "Catalonia").[^23]
- The [Afsharid Dynasty](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Afsharid_Dynasty "Afsharid Dynasty") survived as a rump state in [Mashhad](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mashhad "Mashhad") and surrounding areas after most of its territory in Iran and Khorasan conquered by the [Zands](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Zand_Dynasty "Zand Dynasty") and [Durrani Empire](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Durrani_Empire "Durrani Empire"), until the region finally annexed by the [Qajars](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Qajar_Dynasty "Qajar Dynasty") in 1796.

### Modern history

- The modern country of [Luxembourg](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Luxembourg "Luxembourg") is the rump state of the former [Duchy of Luxembourg](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Duchy_of_Luxembourg "Duchy of Luxembourg"), which lost two-thirds of its territory due to [multiple partitions](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Partitions_of_Luxembourg "Partitions of Luxembourg") between 1659 and 1839. This was cemented by the [Treaty of London](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Treaty_of_London_\(1839\) "Treaty of London (1839)"), which gave most of its former territory to newly independent [Belgium](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Belgium "Belgium").[^24]
- [The Vatican City](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Vatican_City "Vatican City") can be considered a rump state of the [Papal States](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Papal_States "Papal States") (756–1870). The Papal States fell in 1870 when [Rome was captured by Italy](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Capture_of_Rome "Capture of Rome"). The pope was confined to a small area known as the Vatican hill, which included [St Peter’s basilica](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/St._Peter%27s_Basilica "St. Peter's Basilica") and walled off from the rest of Italy. In 1929, The Lateran treaty was signed which formed the Vatican City as an independent state with jurisdiction and authority of the [Roman Catholic Church](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Catholic_Church "Catholic Church") keeping many Papal state institutions as remnants.[^25]
- The modern-day state of [Brunei](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Brunei "Brunei") is a rump state of the former [Bruneian Sultanate (1368–1888)](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bruneian_Sultanate_\(1368%E2%80%931888\) "Bruneian Sultanate (1368–1888)"), which once encompassed much of northern [Borneo](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Borneo "Borneo"). The nation declined sharply during the 19th century, eventually falling under a British protectorate [^26] and reduced to its present size by 1901. Brunei would ultimately regain its independence in 1984, remaining a small remnant of the former empire still ruled by the [House of Bolkiah](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/House_of_Bolkiah "House of Bolkiah"), which has governed the nation throughout almost its entire existence.
- The [Republic of German-Austria](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Republic_of_German-Austria "Republic of German-Austria") was created in 1918 as the initial rump state for areas with a predominantly German-speaking population within what had been the [Austro-Hungarian Empire](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Austro-Hungarian_Empire "Austro-Hungarian Empire").[^27]
- The [Republic of Armenia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/First_Republic_of_Armenia "First Republic of Armenia") became a rump state in 1920 following the Ankara Government victory in the [Turkish–Armenian War](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Turkish%E2%80%93Armenian_War "Turkish–Armenian War").[^28] [^29] [^30]
- In 1918–1919, after World War I, a succession of several short-lived rump states existed within the historical territory of Hungary: the [First Hungarian Republic](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/First_Hungarian_Republic "First Hungarian Republic") (1918–1919), the [Hungarian Soviet Republic](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hungarian_Soviet_Republic "Hungarian Soviet Republic") (March – August 1919),[^31] the [Hungarian Republic](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hungarian_Republic_\(1919%E2%80%931920\) "Hungarian Republic (1919–1920)").[^32]
- The [Second Czechoslovak Republic](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Second_Czechoslovak_Republic "Second Czechoslovak Republic") was the result of the events following the [Munich Agreement](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Munich_Agreement "Munich Agreement"), where [Czechoslovakia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Czechoslovakia "Czechoslovakia") was forced to cede the German-populated [Sudetenland](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sudetenland "Sudetenland") region to [Germany](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Nazi_Germany "Nazi Germany") on 1 October 1938. The state existed for 169 days during which it lost the region of [Carpathian Ruthenia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Carpathian_Ruthenia "Carpathian Ruthenia").[^33]
- In 1940, [Vichy France](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Vichy_France "Vichy France") became a rump state of France after the [fall of France](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Battle_of_France "Battle of France"). While northern France and the Atlantic coast were occupied by [Germany](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Nazi_Germany "Nazi Germany"), Vichy France's unoccupied territory was reduced to the south with Vichy as the administrative capital during the Period and [Philippe Petain](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Philippe_P%C3%A9tain "Philippe Pétain") as head of state. Germany soon would occupy all of France while the Vichy government still remained in place. In 1944, the regime went into exile in [Sigmaringen](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sigmaringen "Sigmaringen") as the [exterritorial](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Extraterritoriality "Extraterritoriality") [Sigmaringen enclave](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sigmaringen_enclave "Sigmaringen enclave") until it was officially abolished in August 1944.[^34]
- In September 1943, the [Kingdom of Italy](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kingdom_of_Italy "Kingdom of Italy") was reduced to a rump state, then nicknamed the [Kingdom of the South](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kingdom_of_the_South "Kingdom of the South"), while the [Italian Social Republic](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Italian_Social_Republic "Italian Social Republic"), a [Nazi-installed puppet state](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/List_of_World_War_II_puppet_states#Germany "List of World War II puppet states"), controlled most of its territory. With [Allied](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Allies_of_World_War_II "Allies of World War II") support, the Kingdom of Italy gradually regained control over Italian territory, achieving victory in April 1945.[^35] [^36] [^37]
- On April 30, 1945, [Adolf Hitler](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Adolf_Hitler "Adolf Hitler") killed himself. [Karl Dönitz](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Karl_D%C3%B6nitz "Karl Dönitz"), who succeeded him as [President of Germany](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/President_of_Germany_\(1919%E2%80%931945\) "President of Germany (1919–1945)"), appointed [Joseph Goebbels](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Joseph_Goebbels "Joseph Goebbels") as [Chancellor](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/List_of_chancellors_of_Germany#Nazi_Germany_\(1933%E2%80%931945\) "List of chancellors of Germany"), only for Goebbels himself to die by suicide, avoiding capture by the Allies, the following day. Resultantly, Dönitz formed the [Flensburg Government](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Flensburg_Government "Flensburg Government"), led by [Lutz von Krosigk](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lutz_Graf_Schwerin_von_Krosigk "Lutz Graf Schwerin von Krosigk"). It was arrested on May 22, and officially dissolved by the [Berlin Declaration](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Berlin_Declaration_\(1945\) "Berlin Declaration (1945)") on June 5.
- The [Federal Republic of Yugoslavia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Federal_Republic_of_Yugoslavia "Federal Republic of Yugoslavia") (1992–2003) / [Serbia and Montenegro](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Serbia_and_Montenegro "Serbia and Montenegro") (2003–2006) was often viewed as the rump state left behind by the [Socialist Federal Republic of Yugoslavia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Socialist_Federal_Republic_of_Yugoslavia "Socialist Federal Republic of Yugoslavia") (1945–1992) after it [broke up](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Breakup_of_Yugoslavia "Breakup of Yugoslavia").[^38] SFR Yugoslavia itself was considered the 'rump Yugoslavia' for its last ten months, between [Slovenian](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Independence_of_Slovenia "Independence of Slovenia") and [Croatian declarations of independence](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Independence_of_Croatia "Independence of Croatia") on 25 June 1991 and [the legal dissolution of Yugoslavia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Constitution_of_the_Federal_Republic_of_Yugoslavia "Constitution of the Federal Republic of Yugoslavia") on 27 April 1992.[^39]
- [Taiwan](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Taiwan "Taiwan") is the rump state of the [Republic of China](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Republic_of_China_\(1912%E2%80%931949\) "Republic of China (1912–1949)") left over after the [retreat](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Retreat_of_the_government_of_the_Republic_of_China_to_Taiwan "Retreat of the government of the Republic of China to Taiwan") from the mainland.[^40] The current [status of Taiwan](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Political_status_of_Taiwan "Political status of Taiwan") is disputed and varies based on the observer's perspective.[^41]
- The [Republic of Turkey](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Republic_of_Turkey "Republic of Turkey") used to be a rump state left over in [Anatolia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Anatolia "Anatolia") after the [dissolution of the Ottoman Empire](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dissolution_of_the_Ottoman_Empire "Dissolution of the Ottoman Empire") and the consequent loss of its territory in Northern Africa, the Middle East, and Europe (amounting to 89% of its former size).[^42]
- The [Democratic Republic of Yemen](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Democratic_Republic_of_Yemen "Democratic Republic of Yemen") was the rump state of [South Yemen](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/South_Yemen "South Yemen"); established during the [1994 Yemeni civil war](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Yemeni_civil_war_\(1994\) "Yemeni civil war (1994)"), it eventually collapsed once the Government retook control.
- Shortly after the onset of the [First Libyan Civil War](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Libyan_civil_war_\(2011\) "Libyan civil war (2011)"), the [Great Socialist People's Libyan Arab Jamahiriya](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/History_of_Libya_under_Muammar_Gaddafi#Great_Socialist_People's_Libyan_Arab_Jamahiriya_\(1977%E2%80%932011\) "History of Libya under Muammar Gaddafi") was rapidly reduced to a rump state as rebel offensives progressed.[^43] [^44] Following the [Battle of Tripoli](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Battle_of_Tripoli_\(2011\) "Battle of Tripoli (2011)") in August, the state's territorial decline accelerated, eventually being reduced to the city of [Sirte](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sirte "Sirte") during the [Second Gulf of Sidra offensive](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Second_Gulf_of_Sidra_offensive "Second Gulf of Sidra offensive"). Following [Muammar Gaddafi](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Muammar_Gaddafi "Muammar Gaddafi") 's [killing](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Killing_of_Muammar_Gaddafi "Killing of Muammar Gaddafi") during the [Battle of Sirte](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Battle_of_Sirte_\(2011\) "Battle of Sirte (2011)"), the last remnants of the state were dissolved as Libya was declared "liberated." [^45]
- Until the [fall of Damascus](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Fall_of_Damascus_\(2024\) "Fall of Damascus (2024)") on 8 December 2024, the remaining territory once held by [Ba'athist Syria](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ba%27athist_Syria "Ba'athist Syria") consisted of [Damascus](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Damascus "Damascus") (encircled by the rebels) and three western coastal [Alawite](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Alawite "Alawite") -majority governorates of [Latakia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Latakia_Governorate "Latakia Governorate") and [Tartus](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tartus_Governorate "Tartus Governorate") which were part of the historical [Alawite State](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Alawite_State "Alawite State").[^46]
