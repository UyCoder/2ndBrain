**Macau–mainland China relations** refer to the relationship between [mainland China](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mainland_China "Mainland China") and [Macau](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Macau "Macau"). In 1999, Portugal [transferred sovereignty over Macau](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Handover_of_Macau "Handover of Macau") to the [People's Republic of China](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/China "China") and established a [special administrative region](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Special_administrative_regions_of_China "Special administrative regions of China") in accordance with the [Sino-Portuguese Joint Declaration](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sino-Portuguese_Joint_Declaration "Sino-Portuguese Joint Declaration"). Macau enjoys a high degree of autonomy in accordance with the [Basic Law of Macao](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Macao_Basic_Law "Macao Basic Law").

## Cooperation

The Outline of the 12th Five-Year Plan for National Economic and Social Development of the People's Republic of China, promulgated in March 2011, clearly stated the country's support for deepening economic cooperation between the Mainland and Macau, the continued implementation of [CEPA](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mainland_and_Hong_Kong_Closer_Economic_Partnership_Arrangement "Mainland and Hong Kong Closer Economic Partnership Arrangement"), and defined the important functions of [Guangdong](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Guangdong "Guangdong") -Macau cooperation in the Framework Agreement on Guangdong-Macao Cooperation. At present, the [Hong Kong](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hong_Kong "Hong Kong") Government has the following platforms for the HKSAR Government to participate in regional co-operation in the Mainland:

1. The [Guangdong](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Guangdong "Guangdong") -Macao Cooperation Joint Conference;
2. [Zhuhai](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Zhuhai "Zhuhai") -Macao Cooperation Conference;
3. Pan- [Pearl River Delta](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pearl_River_Delta "Pearl River Delta") Regional Cooperation and Development Forum;
4. [Beijing](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Beijing "Beijing") -Macao Economic and Trade Co-operation Conference;
5. [Hong Kong](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hong_Kong "Hong Kong") and Macao Co-operation High Level Meeting and related contents; (See [Hong Kong–Macau Relations](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hong_Kong%E2%80%93Macau_relations "Hong Kong–Macau relations"))

Through these platforms, Macao and the Mainland can carry out co-operation in areas such as cross-boundary infrastructure, facilitation measures to facilitate the flow of people and goods, business and investment promotion, environmental protection, food safety, information technology, urban development, tourism cooperation, exchange of cultural activities, promotion of sports events, and training of rehabilitation personnel.
