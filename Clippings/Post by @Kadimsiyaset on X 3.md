If you didn't succeed on the first try, consider yourself lucky. Effortless excellence is a poor teacher and a treacherous friend.

The successes we savor most begin with a struggle and end with a mastery hard-won through difficulty.

Success becomes both sweeter and more repeatable when it is the product of conscious effort.
